Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help.

30-Day Window

You have 30 days from delivery to return items for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Free Returns

All returns are free within Colombia. We'll email you a prepaid return label to make the process as easy as possible.

Simple Process

Start your return online in just a few clicks. No lengthy forms or complicated procedures - we keep it simple.

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return or exchange your purchase

1
Start Return

Log into your account and select "Return Item" from your order history, or contact our support team.

2
Get Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours. Print it out and attach to your package.

3
Pack & Ship

Pack items in original packaging (or any secure box) and drop off at any authorized shipping location.

4
Get Refund

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.
