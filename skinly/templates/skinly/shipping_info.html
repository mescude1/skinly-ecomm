Shipping FAQ

Common questions about our shipping process

Orders are processed Monday through Friday. Orders placed before 2 PM are typically processed the same day, while orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends are processed the next business day. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Currently, we only ship within Colombia. We're working on expanding our international shipping options and hope to serve customers worldwide soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Our customer service team is available to help with any tracking questions.

All our packages are insured and carefully packed to prevent damage. If your package arrives damaged or doesn't arrive at all, please contact us within 48 hours at support@skinly.co. We'll investigate and arrange for a replacement or full refund.

You can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our customer service team. Once your order has been processed and shipped, we cannot change the delivery address. Please double-check your shipping information before completing your purchase.
